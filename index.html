We meet you where you need us.

Ability Physio NW is a mobile physiotherapy service for children and adults with a disability living on the North West Coast of Tasmania (from Smithton to Latrobe).

We can visit clients in their homes, school, childcare centre, or any other setting that is relevant to the client’s needs.

Contact Us

Experienced health professionals.

Ability Physio NW specialises in the management of conditions such as Cerebral Palsy, Down Syndrome, acquired Brain Injury and other neurological conditions.
Contact Us

Dedicated to work closely with your family.

We aim to work closely with the family to establish functional mobility goals.

This will likely involve setting up a therapy program that can be incorporated into the individuals and family’s daily routine. We aim to support families and carers by providing them with the skills and confidence to care for the physical needs of their child or individual with a disability.
